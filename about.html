I'm a UI Designer & developer originally from Seattle, Wa, USA, living in Berlin since 2013.

I bring my understanding of the complex process required to design and create a product.
In my design work, I research the needs of the customer in order to design and develop
a product they enjoy using and meets their needs. The same principles apply to digital
products and it feels natural for me to design & develop products for mobile and web.

As an in-house designer and working with product stakeholders, I've learned the importance
of listening and understanding while discussing the needs and requirements of a design.
Using my creativity, I work to transform ideas into appealing designs that
are harmonious and easy to use.
